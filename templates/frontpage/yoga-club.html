Yoga Club

Yoga club conducts programs to staff and students to understand the importance of maintaining good health both physically and mentally. Yoga club spreads awareness about the importance of yoga among the students as well as staff members. The club conducts various yoga activities for students and staff members to share their body and mind to conscious and sub-conscious levels. We actively work on yoga principles and try to find a ray of wisdom which brings-in real happiness and inner peace in our hearts.
