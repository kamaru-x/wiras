Sports

The sports facilities at WIRAS College are extensive and cater to a wide range of athletic pursuits. The college boasts well-maintained fields and courts, providing opportunities for various sports such as football, cricket, volleyball, and athletics. The facilities are equipped with high-quality sports equipment, ensuring an enjoyable and competitive experience for students. Additionally, there are dedicated spaces for indoor sports like badminton and open gymnasium for fitness enthusiasts. The sports facilities encourage physical activity, teamwork, and healthy competition, promoting a well-rounded and active lifestyle among the college community.
