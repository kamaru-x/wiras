Alumni Association

The WIRAS Alumni Association is an organization formed by graduates of WIRAS to foster a sense of community among former students. It serves as a platform for networking, professional development, and social connections among alumni. The association organizes various events such as reunions, career fairs, mentorship programs, and guest lectures, allowing alumni to stay connected with each other and with the institute. Through these activities, the WIRAS Alumni Association aims to support the personal and professional growth of its members while promoting a lifelong bond with the institution.
