National Service Scheme (NSS)

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a voluntary program implemented at WIRAS College, where students are encouraged to actively engage in community service and contribute to social initiatives. Through NSS, students have the opportunity to develop their leadership skills, enhance their understanding of social issues, and foster a spirit of empathy and responsibility towards the community. They participate in various activities such as organizing awareness campaigns, conducting health camps, promoting environmental sustainability, and supporting underprivileged sections of society. NSS in WIRAS College aims to nurture socially conscious individuals who can make a positive impact on society through their active involvement and contributions.
