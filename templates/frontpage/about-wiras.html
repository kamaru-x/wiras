Wadihuda Institute of Research and Advanced Studies - (WIRAS)

Founded in 2010, the Wadihuda Institute of Research and Advanced Studies (WIRAS) is affiliated to Kannur University and recognised by the Govt. of Kerala. It is a premier institute of the Ta'aleemul Islam Trust, a non-profit trust with the aim of providing quality education to the students of all sections of the society. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in various disciplines, including Arts, Science, Commerce and Management. The 11-acre campus in the vicinity of NH 66 provides a serene atmosphere conducive to academic, intellectual and physical pursuits.
