Equal Opportunity and SC/ST Cell

The aim of the Equal Opportunity and SC/ST cell of the institute is to provide equal opportunity in education and employment so as to improve their social and educational status. It helps them to take their rightful place in the mainstream of society. While establishing the higher educational institution, the management has adopted policies of the government in recruiting and admitting the students from the SC-ST sections. The policies adopted in the institution comply with the regulations laid by UGC.

Internal Complaints Committee (ICC)

The internal complaint committee has been established according to provisions of “The Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act 2013”. WIRAS has committed itself to providing a congenial and conducive atmosphere in which students, teachers, and non-teaching staff can work together in an environment free of violence, harassment, exploitation, and intimidation.

Placement and Career Counselling Cell

The Placement and Career Counselling Cell at WIRAS College is a dedicated department that assists students in their career exploration, professional development, and job placement endeavors. The cell provides comprehensive guidance and support to help students make informed career choices and enhance their employability skills. It offers a wide range of services, including career counseling sessions, resume building workshops, mock interviews, and job search assistance. The Placement and Career Counselling Cell also liaises with companies and organizations to facilitate campus placements, internships, and job opportunities for students.

Civil Services Academy

The Civil Service Academy at WIRAS College is an institution dedicated to providing comprehensive preparation for civil service exams. It offers specialized training and resources to help students acquire the knowledge and skills required for various civil service positions. The academy provides a structured curriculum covering subjects such as general knowledge, current affairs, reasoning, and aptitude. Experienced faculty members and mentors guide students through the exam preparation process, offering valuable insights, study materials, and practice tests.

Young Leaders Academy

The Leaders Academy at WIRAS College is a specialized program designed to cultivate leadership skills and qualities among students. It provides a unique opportunity for individuals to develop their leadership potential and make a positive impact both within the college community and in the wider world. The Leaders Academy offers a range of activities and initiatives, including workshops, seminars, guest lectures, and experiential learning opportunities. Through these experiences, students gain valuable insights into leadership theories and practices, enhance their communication and problem-solving skills, and learn to inspire and motivate others. The program also emphasizes ethical leadership, social responsibility, and the development of a strong moral compass. By participating in the Leaders Academy, students are equipped with the necessary tools and knowledge to become effective leaders in their chosen fields and make meaningful contributions to society.
