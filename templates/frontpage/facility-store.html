Cooperative Store

The Wiras Cooperative Store, located conveniently on campus, is the go-to destination for all your campus needs. Offering a diverse range of products, from textbooks and stationery to snacks and personal care items, we prioritize competitive pricing without compromising quality. With a student-centric approach, we actively seek feedback to tailor our offerings to meet the demands of the Wiras community. Our commitment to sustainability is reflected in our promotion of eco-friendly products and collaboration with ethical suppliers. When you visit the Wiras Cooperative Store, you'll experience convenience, affordability, and a vibrant sense of community, making it the ideal place to meet all your college shopping requirements.
