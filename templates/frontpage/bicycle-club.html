Bicycle Club

WIRAS Bicycle Club unites biking enthusiasts from all levels within the college, organizing rides and events that promote cycling and create a supportive community. From leisurely campus rides to thrilling trail adventures, the club offers diverse cycling experiences. By fostering friendships, knowledge sharing, and a love for biking, the club contributes to a healthier, greener, and more connected campus environment.
