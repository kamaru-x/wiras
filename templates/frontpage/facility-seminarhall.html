Seminar Hall

The Wiras College Seminar Hall stands as a dynamic space that inspires intellectual growth, fosters collaboration, and hosts a diverse range of engaging events. Equipped with cutting-edge technology and flexible seating arrangements, our Seminar Hall offers a platform for discussions, presentations, workshops, and seminars. With its adaptable seating configurations, the hall can accommodate various event formats, promoting interaction and active participation. The Wiras College Seminar Hall provides a stimulating ambiance, fostering an environment conducive to focused learning and creative exchange. Whether it's a renowned guest speaker, faculty-led seminar, or student-led initiative, the Seminar Hall serves as a catalyst for knowledge exchange, collaboration, and transformative learning experiences within the Wiras College community.
